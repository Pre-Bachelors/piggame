Rules

During your turn, you can take two possible paths:

  • Roll the die - if you roll 1, your turn total becomes 0 and it becomes your opponent's turn; otherwise, you add the amount you rolled to your turn total.
  • Hold - your turn total is added to your points and your turn ends.

The first player to reach 100 points wins!
